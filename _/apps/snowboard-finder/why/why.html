Why these boards?

It is important to getthe right snowboard.

Length

A longer board is more stable while a shorter board is more manuverable. The trick is finding the right balance in the middle and then tweaking it to your riding style.

At _______ feet and _______ inches a board length of _______ cm will give you a nice ride on the hill

Width

A bigger foot needs a bigger board. If your board is to narrow you will get toe or heel drag. A wider board will float in the deep snow better but if it is too wide it will be hard to go edge to edge.
