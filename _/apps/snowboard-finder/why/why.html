Length

A longer board will ride more stable, while a shorter board will be more maneuverable. With your specs, we can help you find the right balance, and tweak it to your riding style.

Width

A bigger foot requires a bigger board width underfoot. If your board is too narrow your toes and heels will drag, likely throwing off your balance. While a wide board will float in deep snow, a board too wide will hinder your ability to make ripping fast, precise, toe and heel edge turns.
